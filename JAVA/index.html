J A Vaghela & Associates

We are Chartered Accountants firm based in Rajkot, Gujarat, India registered with The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI), a statutory body established by an Act of Parliament, viz. The Chartered Accountants Act, 1949 (Act No.XXXVIII of 1949) for regulating the profession of Chartered Accountancy in the country.

Our mission has been to render professional services of the highest standard to clients, upholding the code of conduct and ethics of the profession. Keeping these grounded principles in consideration, we continue to grow and expand in our endeavour to exceed expectations.

CA Janak Vaghela, founder of the firm, is member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI). Under his leadership firm is dedicated to provide service of highest standard to the clients. Despite his young age, his vision and commitment are leading firm to the tremendous growth.
